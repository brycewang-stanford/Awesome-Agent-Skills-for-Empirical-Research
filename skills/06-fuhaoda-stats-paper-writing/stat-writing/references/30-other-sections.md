# Other sections: Appendix, Acknowledgements, Supplement, References

## Appendix
Use appendices for:
- long proofs / derivations,
- additional simulation tables,
- extra implementation details,
- material that supports the paper but is not essential for the main narrative.

Guidelines:
- Keep appendices organised (A, B, ...), each with a clear purpose.
- Refer to appendices in the main text when relevant.

## Acknowledgements
- Thank contributors who do not meet authorship criteria.
- Disclose funding sources and any conflicts of interest (if required by the venue).

## Online supplement / supplementary material
- Put extra experiments, extended proofs, or data/code documentation here when the venue allows.
- Make sure the core claims remain supported in the main paper.

## References section
- Ensure every listed item is cited in the paper.
- Prefer consistent BibTeX formatting and a stable key convention.
- Check for missing fields (pages, volume, publisher) and protect capitalisation with braces.
