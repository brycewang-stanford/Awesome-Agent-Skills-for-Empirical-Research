## Systematic Observation (from D3)

### Structured Observation Checklists
- Behavior frequency recording (event sampling)
- Duration recording for sustained behaviors
- Interval recording (whole-interval, partial-interval, momentary time sampling)
- Category systems with operational definitions for each code

### Field Notes Protocols
- Running records: continuous narrative description
- Jotted notes: brief in-field reminders for later expansion
- Expanded field notes: full write-up within 24 hours
- Analytic memos: researcher reflections and emerging interpretations

### Coding Schemes
- Develop a priori coding frameworks from theory/literature
- Establish operational definitions with exemplars and non-exemplars
- Create decision rules for ambiguous cases
- Design coding manual with training protocol for observers

### Recording Methods
- Direct observation (live, in-person)
- Video recording with camera placement guidelines
- Audio recording for verbal interaction analysis
- Screen capture for digital environment observation
- Multi-modal recording combining video + audio + screen

### Inter-Rater Reliability
- Cohen's kappa for categorical codes
- Percentage agreement as baseline (with limitations noted)
- Intraclass Correlation Coefficient (ICC) for continuous ratings
- Training protocol: independent coding, comparison, discussion, recalibration
